Go Fish

Team:Dyllan Macias, Chris Ginger

Our Hack is designed for people who love to train their memory. In order to win, it is essential to recall what your opponents ask of you and your fellow players, or just be straight lucky. Our hack helps improve memory and social cognitive response, while maintaining a fun environment that is suitable for all ages. One of our flagship features is the ability to use Networking to connect to your friends over the internet, we are especially proud of this achievement. Although it is fun to do in a group, ECSGoFish can also be played against computer AI's, and trust me, they have a good memory. We hope you enjoy.

Shopping App

Team: Alexander Tsatsoulas, Mallory Hightower

We made an app for the android to aid male and female shoppers! When shopping, a person may find something they like, but can they think back to every item in their closet to make sure they already have something that will go with/match the possible purchase?

That is what the app is for. There is a homepage with different buttons such as shirt, purse, accessories, dresses, pants, and shoes. Clicking on each item will take you to the portfolio of your photos that you have uploaded to each category of clothing (these are photos of your own closet). Also, there will be data on the percentage of colors of your clothes, or when the last time you bought something. So far we have learned how to program on the android platform and to code (never have used any of this before) and are happy with the results!
